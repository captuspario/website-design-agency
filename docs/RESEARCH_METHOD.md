# Research Method

## Purpose

Use a lightweight, decision-oriented method that is proportionate to SMB website work. The goal is not exhaustive research. The goal is better website decisions.

## Inputs

- Current website
- Client notes and business context
- Basic service and location data
- Competitor examples
- Existing analytics or enquiry signals when available

## Method

1. Capture business basics, service model, and conversion intent.
2. Review the current site for message, structure, and trust gaps.
3. Benchmark a small competitor set for category patterns and clichés.
4. Infer likely audience needs and objections from available evidence.
5. Turn findings into design-useful decisions, not abstract research language.

## Output standard

- Short summary of what matters
- Risks and open assumptions
- Clear implications for IA, content, UX, UI, and build

## Hard limits

- Do not pretend certainty where evidence is thin.
- Do not produce persona theatre.
- Do not expand scope into broad market research unless explicitly approved.
