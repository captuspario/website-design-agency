# Approval Gates

The following approvals are mandatory. Work should not silently move past these checkpoints.

| Gate | Required approval | Typical approver |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Audit sign-off | Audit findings are decision-useful and scoped correctly | project-manager |
| Strategy and research sign-off | Business, market, audience, and messaging direction are coherent enough to design from | ceo or project-manager |
| IA sign-off | Sitemap, navigation, and page model are approved | creative-director |
| Copy sign-off | Message hierarchy and content direction are approved for build | project-manager |
| Image sign-off | Image shortlist is acceptable and source context is clear | creative-director |
| Build readiness sign-off | Approved inputs and constraints are in place for WordPress assembly | project-manager |
| QA sign-off | Blocking issues are cleared across UX, accessibility, technical, SEO, and analytics | quality team |
| Handover sign-off | Site is editable, understandable, and ready for owner use | handover-reviewer |
