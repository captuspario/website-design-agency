# Website Design Agency

Website Design Agency is a portable Paperclip company package for internal teams that redesign and rebuild simple SMB service websites.

It is designed for compact audit work, redesign blueprinting, maintainable WordPress assembly, QA, and handover. It is intentionally narrow: no ecommerce, no custom app logic, no multilingual expansion, and no live production editing.

The package is structured so a Paperclip runtime can import the company, its agents, and its skills from markdown files with stable slugs and clear approval gates.

## Goals

- Improve clarity, trust, and conversion for simple SMB service websites.
- Keep strategy, design, build, QA, and handover work structured and reviewable.
- Produce maintainable WordPress outcomes using a constrained block-theme workflow.
