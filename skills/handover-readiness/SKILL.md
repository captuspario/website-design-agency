# Handover Readiness

## Purpose

Confirm the final site and its notes are realistic for a non-technical owner to manage after delivery.

## Inputs

- Built site
- QA notes
- Build notes

## Outputs

- Handover readiness assessment
- Owner editing risk list
- Required handover notes

## Steps

1. Review common editing tasks such as text and image changes.
2. Check whether reusable patterns remain understandable in the editor.
3. Identify fragile areas that need guidance or cleanup.
4. Summarise what the owner must know after handover.

## Done criteria

- Typical owner edits are feasible.
- Fragile areas are visible.
- Handover notes are specific enough to be useful.

## Escalation

Escalate when the site cannot realistically be maintained without specialist intervention.
