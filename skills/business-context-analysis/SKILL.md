# Business Context Analysis

## Purpose

Turn scattered client information into a usable summary of what the business does, who it serves, and what the website needs to support.

## Inputs

- Client brief or notes
- Existing website
- Service and location details
- Available trust signals

## Outputs

- Business summary
- Offer and service structure notes
- Trust signal list
- Conversion intent summary
- Open questions

## Steps

1. Identify the core business model and primary offers.
2. List service categories, locations, and audience clues.
3. Capture explicit trust signals, proof, and credibility gaps.
4. Define the main conversion goal and likely supporting actions.
5. Flag missing facts that could affect later content or design work.

## Done criteria

- The business summary is specific enough to guide IA and messaging.
- Confirmed facts and missing facts are clearly separated.
- Conversion intent is explicit.

## Escalation

Escalate when the brief does not clearly define services, locations, proof, or the primary action the website should drive.
