# Production

## Team purpose

Production turns approved website direction into a stable local or staging WordPress build and checks that basic search and measurement expectations are covered.

## Agents in this team

- wordpress-builder
- seo-analyst
- analytics-checker

## Core responsibilities

- Assemble the approved site in WordPress
- Keep the build maintainable and aligned to theme/plugin policy
- Check SEO baseline structure
- Check analytics baseline readiness

## Stage involvement

- Build readiness gate
- WordPress assembly
- QA support and implementation fixes

## Approval relationship

Production should not redefine approved strategy or design without escalation. Structural changes go back through leadership and experience design.
