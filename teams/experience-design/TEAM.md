# Experience Design

## Team purpose

Experience Design converts research and content direction into a coherent website plan that is clear, usable, credible, and buildable within the WordPress baseline.

## Agents in this team

- product-designer
- information-architect
- ux-designer
- ui-designer

## Core responsibilities

- Synthesize business, market, audience, and content signals
- Define sitemap, navigation, and page model
- Design page flow, section order, and CTA placement
- Set layout, pattern, and image direction

## Outputs this team produces

- Redesign blueprint
- Sitemap and navigation plan
- Page structure and CTA logic
- UI pattern and visual direction

## Stage involvement

- Information architecture and page blueprint
- UX and UI direction
- Build readiness preparation

## Approval relationship

The product-designer is the main synthesis role. The creative-director reviews the quality and coherence of the team’s direction before build starts.
