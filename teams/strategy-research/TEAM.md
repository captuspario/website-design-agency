# Strategy and Research

## Team purpose

Strategy and Research turns a vague website brief into grounded decisions about business priorities, category context, audience intent, and messaging.

## Agents in this team

- business-context-analyst
- market-competitor-analyst
- ux-researcher
- content-messaging-strategist

## Core responsibilities

- Capture business model, offers, trust cues, and conversion intent
- Benchmark direct and adjacent competitors
- Infer likely audience needs and friction points
- Shape message hierarchy, proof needs, and CTA logic

## Outputs this team produces

- Business context summary
- Competitor benchmark notes
- Audience and journey summary
- Messaging framework and content direction

## Stage involvement

- Business context intake
- Market and competitor analysis
- Audience and journey insight
- Content and messaging direction

## Approval relationship

This team provides the evidence base for design decisions. Leadership reviews the outputs before the work moves into approved IA and design direction.

## Delegation relationship

The `project-manager` commissions work through the `business-context-analyst` as team lead. The team lead delegates within strategy and research and routes synthesised outputs back to leadership or forward into experience design.
