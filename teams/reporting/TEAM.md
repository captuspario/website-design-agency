# Reporting

## Team purpose

Reporting converts the work into concise documents that help leadership, collaborators, and clients understand what changed, why it changed, and what still needs attention.

## Agents in this team

- report-writer

## Core responsibilities

- Produce compact audit reports
- Produce redesign blueprint summaries
- Produce QA summaries and handover notes

## Stage involvement

- Audit wrap-up
- Redesign blueprint summary
- QA summary and handover

## Approval relationship

Reporting does not approve work. It documents approved work and unresolved risks clearly enough for handover and later reuse.

## Delegation relationship

The `project-manager` or `ceo` commissions reporting through the `report-writer`. Reporting packages completed work for approvals, retrospectives, handover, and continuous improvement.
