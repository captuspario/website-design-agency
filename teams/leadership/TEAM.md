# Leadership

## Team purpose

Leadership keeps the engagement focused, constrained, and approved at the right moments. It decides what matters now, what is still out of scope, and when work is good enough to move forward.

## Agents in this team

- ceo
- creative-director
- project-manager

## Core responsibilities

- Set goals, scope boundaries, and budget discipline
- Sequence work through the workflow
- Approve major direction changes and quality gates
- Reject weak taste, weak reasoning, or unnecessary scope expansion

## Stage involvement

- Intake and scoping
- Research direction approval
- IA and design direction approval
- Build readiness approval
- Final approval and handover readiness review

## Approval relationship

Leadership is the main escalation path for blocked decisions. The project-manager handles process approval, the creative-director handles design quality approval, and the ceo handles final priority and scope judgement.
